Arugula Salad with Stone Fruit

Description

This juicy, vibrant arugula salad features fresh cherries, peaches, and nectarines for a summery flavor. For an extra touch of sweetness, opt for rosé vinegar, a rosé wine-based vinegar with a bright, fruity flavor.

ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra-vergin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 11/2 cups red cherry tomatoes
  • 1 arugula
  • 3/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 nectarines, sliced
  • 1 large white peach, sliced
  • 1 cup rainier, pitted and halved
  • 1/2 teaspoon flaky seas salt

Steps

  1. Wisk together olive oil, vinegar, salt and pepper in a small bowl for the dressing
  2. Arrange tomatoes, argula, basil, nectarines and peach slices on a large platter
  3. Drizzle with half the dressing, Top with cherries, seas salt and remaining dressing
  4. Serve immediately
